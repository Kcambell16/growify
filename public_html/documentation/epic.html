Executive Summary

Growify is an application that offers gardeners suggestions based on current and historical weather data as well as local climate and growing conditions. Gardeners will also receive tailored data based on their own list of herbs and vegetables.

Personas

Persona #1

  • Alice Johnson, age 32
  • It is early February, and Alice wants to start a vegetable garden with her 4 year old daughter. She has no experience with planting a garden, so she is looking for resources to help her plan.
  • Profession: Customer Service Representative
  • Technology: Alice has an Android phone, PC, and Kindle. She is tech savvy and enjoys using her devices to make her life easier.
  • Attitudes and Behaviors: Alice is enthusiastic about starting her garden and wants to get a good plan in place before starting. She is short on time, so she is looking for quick and easy ways to start her garden.
  • Frustrations and Needs: Alice wants to create a small garden and would like her daughter to get involved with the process. She needs to select easy to grow plants that don't need a lot of maintenance. In her backyard, she has a medium-sized garden box with plenty of direct sunlight. She will need to be strategic in selecting the right herbs and vegetables since they will all be planted in one garden box.
  • Goals: Alice wants to figure out --
    • when she should begin planting
    • what group of herbs and vegetables grow well together
    • how to maintain her garden
    • when she should harvest
  • Use Case: Alice finds the Growify app on her Android phone. She downloads it and begins to scroll through the various choices of plants to grow. She has 3 top vegetables she would like to grow and finds them in the app database. She sees which ones grow well together and finalizes her list. She finds out when she can start planting and goes out to buy the seed packets necessary to make this garden happen.

User Stories:

  • As a gardener, I want to find plants that grow well together.
  • As a gardener, I want to find out when to harvest.
  • As a gardener, I want to discover when to start planting.
  • As a gardener, I want to access the Growify app on my phone.
  • As a busy gardener, I want an app that is easy and fast to use.

Persona 2

  • Age: 28

  • Technology: Shiloh has a Microsoft Kin (the most hipster of phones) and a Lenovo Thinkpad running Gentoo Linux. He uses Opera to browse the web.
  • Attitudes and Behaviors: March has just begun and Shiloh wants to plant an herb garden for his artisanal herbed goat cheese. He has a fair amount of experience when it comes to growing his herbs, but is always open to new ideas that might improve his yield. Shiloh has already chosen the herbs that he wants to plant. He has spent a lot of time researching their flavor profiles and is confident in his choices. He is very meticulous in his research and may not be keen on using a new resource (our site) that he is unsure of.
  • Frustrations and Needs: Shiloh gets frustrated with using web apps that are difficult to navigate or have a cluttered interface. He needs a fairly minimalistic user interface to be comfortable.
  • Goals: Shiloh want to learn, at the very least --
    • if certain herbs would benefit from sprouting indoors
    • would certain herbs be beneficial to grow with others
    • if the weather will make watering unnecessary on certain days
  • Use Case: Shiloh is preparing for spring planting but wants to get some quick research in. Through a quick search on DuckDuckGo, he finds Growify.io and is convinced it is "the bee's knees"(his words, not mine). He creates a new user profile, and after a brief questionnaire, is immediately assaulted with information on optimal growing conditions for herbs tailored to his preferences. Comforted by the abundance of information readily available and the tastefully minimalistic user interface, he settles into his research and prepares for the upcoming planting.
